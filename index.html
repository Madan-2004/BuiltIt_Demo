Student Gymkhana of IIT Indore

The Student Gymkhana of IIT Indore serves as the heart of student governance and extracurricular activities, playing a pivotal role in shaping the vibrant campus culture. It is a student-elected body that represents the collective interests of the student community, ensuring their active participation in institute decisions and fostering a sense of belonging. The Gymkhana operates through its three primary councils: the Cultural Council, which nurtures creativity and artistic expression through events, workshops, and the annual cultural festival, Fluxus; the Technical Council, which encourages innovation, critical thinking, and technical excellence by organizing coding competitions, workshops, and hackathons; and the Sports Council, which promotes physical fitness and team spirit through a variety of sports events, tournaments, and participation in inter-IIT meets. Additionally, the Gymkhana is deeply involved in student welfare, offering support systems, mentorship programs, and leadership opportunities to help students excel both academically and personally. Through its diverse initiatives, the Student Gymkhana ensures a holistic development experience, making IIT Indore not just an institute of academic excellence but also a dynamic, inclusive, and engaging community.
